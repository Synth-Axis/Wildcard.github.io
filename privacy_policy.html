Wildcard Esports's purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Wildcard Esports's visitors use its website. From time to time, Wildcard Esports may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

Wildcard Esports only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below.

5. Cookies
To enrich and perfect your online experience, Wildcard Esports uses "Cookies", similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Wildcard Esports uses cookies to help Wildcard Esports identify and track visitors, their usage of https://www.wildcardesports.gg, and their website access preferences. Wildcard Esports visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Wildcard Esports's websites, with the drawback that certain features of Wildcard Esports's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to Wildcard Esports's use of cookies.
