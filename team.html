WILDCARD PLAYERS

Discover who our raiders are, their title in the guild, their darkest secrets and shameful stories

Synthaxis

Guild Master

This "claimed-to-be" professor managed to push is team into Mythic+ Keystone 20 at the fierce Tazavesh: So'leah's Gambit.

His legend continues to grow as his 12 cheers have rallied his party to improbable victories.

It is yet to be discovered the actual size of his feet, as none of the items show up when put in the slot. Probably has a nail problem.

Proud owner of a perky pug.

Alexa

Co-GM, Officer & Rave Gal

Our fierce Demon Hunter slashed and diced through Battle for Azeroth into Shadowlands, coming out in the World's Top 40 Alliance Havoc Demon Hunters.

It is hard to find her and keep her at one spot. If we take too long chatting, she is gone! However, she is typically late, having to be summoned 476 times during Shadowlands. We do not now if her 123 keys are still visible on the keyboard.

Her looks are always changing and her most used mount is the world famous "Many-Many".

Mr. Xristias

Co-GM, DPS Officer

The one who brings great devastion. Or so he says in Greek. The DPS of this Priest is top notch - NERF this guy, since nerfing priests is proven not to work.

Making new friends comes naturally to this one, having waved 10 times to allies and enemies alike (right - don't ask!) during the 4 Seasons of Shadowlands.

He knows the DBM says "Interrupt" - Really, he does!
